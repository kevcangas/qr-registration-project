This is the homepage

In this app you can consult users, workgroups, supervisers and sessions to control an activity that requires people working in a group with a superviser.

Where did the idea come from?

This app was made to solve a problematic that raised in a school where the students are studying in a workgroup while a teacher is with them supervising how they are doing his activities. When the students entry on the workgroup, the teacher registries the time when they started, their names and when they finished, the teacher registries the time. Also, the administration requires the number of students in every workgroup to distribute better the students.
